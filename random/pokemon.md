# Soul Link NuzLocke Pokemon Violet

## Rules
1. Players choice for naming pokemon

### Items
-  Heal items only outside of battle, unless held by pokemon

### Death
- Pokemon dies put it in a death pokemon bank tab, and never use it again.
- Results of death is after the battle
	(example) bobs pokemon dies, my soul linked pokemon wont die till end of battle.

### Fights
- Participants do not have to use soullinked simultaniously at the same gym.
- Do not have to fight same trainer at same time, but we can.

### Mons
- Pokemon can not exceed the highest level pokemon of the gym leader.
- Can catch shiny pokemon, but cant use them.
- You cannot catch legendaries
- Cant catch same pokemon in same area.
- No buy moves, no TMs only moves your monster learns naturally
- lachonk is a tutorial pokemon he cannot be used must go to box.
- can do raids, but cant use the pokemon
